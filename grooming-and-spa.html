Welcome to Our Premier Grooming and Spa Services

At Pet Paradise Pet Care, we make best efforts to provide every pet the best of their look and ensure they feel relaxed and confident. Grooming and Spa services at Pet Paradise offer effective treatments to maintain cleanliness, health, and happiness of your pets. Dedicated team at our facility use high-quality products and gentle techniques to provide a stress-free and enjoyable grooming experience to your pet.

Our Grooming Services

Bathing and Drying

We use pet-friendly products including shampoos and conditioners specially curated to suit the coat type and skin sensitivity of your beloved pet. Bathing includes a gentle cleaning to remove dirt, ticks and loose fur. Bathing follows by a hand-drying using soft towels and gentle blow dryers to ensure the pet receives a comfortable and relaxing experience.

Haircuts and Trimming

We have best of industry skilled stylists and groomers to give your pet a variety of breed-specific cuts and trims, and ensure a precise and professional grooming. We also work with you to create a customized grooming style to address the needs and preferences of your pet and to give them their best look.

Nail Trimming and Paw Care

Our expert groomers provide gentle, precise nail trims and thorough paw care, ensuring your furry friend stays happy, healthy, and comfortable on their feet. We use pet-friendly cleaning products and moisturizers to help maintain healthy and soft paws.

Ear Cleaning and Plucking

We ensure your pet's ear health with our ear cleaning and plucking services. Our expert team gently cleans and plucks excess hair, preventing infections and promoting overall ear-hygiene for a happier, healthier pet.

Teeth Brushing

Optimize your pet's health with our dental-hygiene services. We, at Pet Paradise, ensure clean teeth, fresh breath, and overall well-being through regular, thorough dental care for your beloved pet.


Our Spa Treatments

Luxury Bath Packages

Engage your beloved pet with Aromatherapy and Medicated baths, offering a soothing relaxation and therapeutic benefits for healthier skin and a refreshed and happy pet.

Deep Conditioning Treatments

Facial Treatments

Pamper your pet with Pet Paradise’s gentle facial treatments, tailored to cleanse, rejuvenate, and soothe, leaving their face fresh and radiant.

Massage Therapy

Special Add-Ons
