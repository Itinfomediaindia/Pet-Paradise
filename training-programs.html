Welcome to Our Comprehensive Pet Training Programs

At Pet Paradise, we believe that a well-trained pet is a happy pet. Our professional training programs are designed to help your furry friend develop good manners, social skills, and obedience. Whether you have a new puppy or an older dog needing some behavior modification, our experienced trainers are here to assist you in achieving your training goals.

Get Appointment

Our Training Philosophy

Our training approach is based on positive reinforcement and scientifically proven methods. We focus on building a strong bond between you and your pet through trust and mutual respect. Our goal is to make training a fun and rewarding experience for both you and your pet.


Training Programs Offered

Puppy Training

  • Puppy Basics: Designed for puppies aged 8 weeks to 6 months, this program covers essential skills such as potty training, crate training, basic commands (sit, stay, come), and socialization. We also address common puppy behaviors like nipping and chewing.

  • Puppy Socialization: Socialization is crucial for a well-rounded puppy. This program includes supervised play sessions with other puppies, exposure to different environments, sounds, and people to help your puppy grow into a confident and well-behaved dog.

Obedience Training

  • Basic Obedience: For dogs of all ages, this program focuses on fundamental commands such as sit, stay, come, down, and heel. We also work on leash manners and polite greetings.

  • Advanced Obedience: Building on the basics, this program introduces more complex commands and off-leash training. Your dog will learn to follow commands reliably in various environments with distractions.

Behavior Modification

  • Problem Behaviors: Our behavior modification program addresses specific issues such as excessive barking, jumping, aggression, separation anxiety, and destructive behavior. Our trainers develop customized plans to address these challenges and improve your pet’s behavior.

  • One-on-One Sessions: For personalized attention, we offer one-on-one training sessions tailored to your pet’s unique needs. These sessions allow us to focus on specific behavior issues or advanced training goals.

Specialty Training

  • Agility Training: For dogs with high energy and a love for physical activity, our agility training program introduces your pet to obstacle courses, jumps, tunnels, and more. This is a great way to keep your dog physically fit and mentally stimulated.

  • Trick Training: Have fun with your pet by teaching them new tricks such as roll over, play dead, shake, and fetch. Trick training is a great way to engage your pet and show off their skills to friends and family.

  • Therapy Dog Training: If you’re interested in having your dog become a certified therapy dog, our training program prepares them for the necessary evaluations and certifications. This includes training for calm behavior, socialization, and specific tasks required for therapy work.


Training Methods

Positive Reinforcement

  • We use positive reinforcement techniques, rewarding desired behaviors with treats, praise, and play. This method encourages your pet to repeat good behaviors and creates a positive association with training sessions.

Consistency and Patience

  • Consistency is key in training. Our trainers work with you to ensure that commands and routines are consistent both in and out of training sessions. Patience and understanding are emphasized throughout the process to foster a supportive learning environment.

Customized Plans

  • Every pet is unique, and our training programs are tailored to meet the individual needs of your pet. We take into account their personality, breed characteristics, and any specific challenges they may have.

Training Packages

Group Classes

  • Group classes provide an excellent opportunity for socialization and learning in a structured environment. Classes are kept small to ensure individual attention and to allow for interactive learning.

Private Sessions

  • For personalized training, we offer private sessions that can be conducted at our facility, at your home, or in a location of your choice. These sessions are ideal for addressing specific issues or for pets that may not do well in a group setting.

Board and Train

  • Our board and train program allows your pet to stay with us for intensive training. This is a great option for busy owners or for addressing more challenging behaviors. Your pet will receive daily training sessions and care, with progress updates provided regularly.

Booking and Rates

Easy Scheduling

  • Scheduling a training session is simple. You can book through our website, over the phone, or by visiting our facility. We offer flexible scheduling to accommodate your busy lifestyle.

Competitive Pricing

  • Our training programs are competitively priced, with various packages available to suit your budget. Contact us for detailed pricing information and to learn about our special offers.

Follow-Up Support

  • We provide ongoing support even after the training program is complete. Our trainers are available to answer questions and provide additional guidance to ensure the long-term success of your pet’s training.
